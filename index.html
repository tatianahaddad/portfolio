Pride and Prejudice Quiz App

Impress your friends with your knowledge of Pride and Prejudice trivia by submitting your answer when presented with 10 multiple choice questions one at a time, receiving feedback to know if the answer was correct or not, and receiving your final score results with the option to play again. Making a fun, interactive quiz based on my favorite novel was a no-brainer. I hope that those who play it are filled with happy memories about the greatest love story ever told just as I was when I created it.

HTML was used to load the initial start page and serve as the container for follow-up questions and feedback pages included through the use of JavaScript functions and jQuery libraries. JavaScript functions and jQuery libraries were also used to provide interactive material for the user to click and submit answers. CSS was used throughout to create visually striking content and to allow for a seamless UX.
